Hey! I'm Kristen. I design branding, advertising, front-end, and UX/UI; I'm also a traditional artist and I create illustrations and portraiture primarily with pencils. My goal is to create multifaceted experiences with carefully crafted designs. I've worked with small businesses and large corporations alike. Some of the brands I’ve designed for are Dunkin' Donuts, Party City, and AutoNation.

Artist, Designer, Pug Lover

Long before I discovered my passion for design, I was a STEM student. Working in a research lab and studying biology, I had my entire life planned out as a molecular geneticist. A couple years into college, I was asked to make a website for a campus biology club and I begrudgingly accepted. I hit the internet to learn where to begin and got started creating. Hours flew by and before I knew it, I was flabbergasted! I fell in love! I couldn’t believe it! The person who spent 8 hours a day in a sterile white coat was mesmerized by colors and shapes! I was undeniably enamored with combining elements to create a product that could inspire others and bring people together. I felt like I was on top of the world and I knew then that there was no going back. Still a STEM student at heart, I love solving visual problems with creative logic. The tools I use the most are Photoshop, Proto.io, Brackets, Illustrator, and of course good ol' pencil & paper.
