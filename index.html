Hey! I'm Kristen. I design branding, advertising, front-end, and UX/UI; I'm also a traditional artist and I create illustrations and portraiture primarily with pencils. My goal is to create multifaceted experiences with carefully crafted designs. I've worked with small businesses and large corporations alike. Some of the brands I’ve designed for are Dunkin' Donuts, Party City, and AutoNation.

My philosophy is that easy should be hard. I pursue design that does it's job without you noticing it. Good design not only conveys its message with confidence, but it also does it with brevity and wit. Design not only applies to graphics but to every facet of communication and presentation in art, fashion, movies, literature, and more. Few things are more annoying than not being able to focus on one character in a scene, find the date on a flyer, or when you buy an entire package of raisin oatmeal cookies thinking they’re chocolate chip. The study of design is really the study of people, and boy, is there a lot to learn.

I hope to continue my career in the visual arts and continually search to make the world a more beautiful (or at least less frustrating) place.
