Hey! I'm Kristen. I design branding, advertising, front-end, and UX/UI; I'm also a traditional artist and I create illustrations and portraiture primarily with pencils. My goal is to create multifaceted experiences with carefully crafted designs. I've worked with small businesses and large corporations alike. Some of the brands I’ve designed for are Dunkin' Donuts, Party City, and AutoNation.

Artist, Designer, Pug Expert

Long before I discovered my passion for design design, I was a STEM student; working in a research lab and pursuing a career in molecular genetics, I was happily going through my life as a biologist. A couple years into college I was asked to make a website for a campus science club. I had no idea where to begin so I sat down one night and begrudgingly began to research and create. Hours fly by and before I knew it, I was flabbergasted! I fell in love! I couldn’t believe it! The person who spent 8 hours a day in a sterile white coat was mesmerized by colors and shapes! I became undeniably enamored combining visual elements to create a product that could inspire and bring people together. I felt like I was on top of the world and I knew then that there was no going back. Still a STEM student at heart, I love solving visual problems with creative logic.
