Kristen Hanlon: artist, designer, pug expert

My introduction to design began as a complete mistake. In college I was originally a STEM student, working in a research lab and pursuing a career in molecular genetics. I was happily going through my life when upon being elected as the secretary of a biology club, the responsibility of designing a website and creating a logo was thrust onto me.

Frustrated, I hit the internet to learn where to begin. I begrudgingly started working on a branding package and before I knew it, I was flabbergasted; I fell in love! I couldnâ€™t believe it! The person who spent 8 hours a day in a sterile white coat was mesmerized by colors and shapes! I was undeniably enamored with combining all these elements to create a product that would inspire other students and bring people together. I felt like I was on top of the world and I knew then that there was no going back.

Still a STEM student at heart, I love solving creative visual problems with logic. Take a copy of my resume!
